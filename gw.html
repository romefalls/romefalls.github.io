ST. PASADENA, OREGON, USA.

Once an unassuming little town in Lane County that now serves its purpose as a battleground.



THE M11 INSURGENCY

Nihil amittendum

The M11 insurgency is a militant group that opposes corporate control and government overreach. Their anger, rising against its next-door corporation, led them to gain their de facto trademark of brutality.
A vendetta of necessity.


SYDNEY GRAY

"Never do the wolves expect the sheep to fight back."

The leader of the M11 insurgency. Her mission is simple - when it comes to liberating the United States, leave no drop of blood unspilled.


C.O.R.E.

Qui audet adipiscitur

The Corporate Overreach Resistance Expulsion Act legitimizes the campaign against the M11 insurgency, marking Sydney's forces for death.
A cherry-picked force of special operatives from the world's most elite red, blue, and gold units.


JULIUS LEVITAN

"Never is there a moment that I'm not proud to be on the right side of history."

Chairman of the Washington Corporation. The world's most powerful person. The pen against Sydney's sword.
